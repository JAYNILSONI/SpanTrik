Unlocking the new area of space economy by taking India into space tourism.

SPANTRIK Private Limited, established in 2020, manufactures rockets and provides commercial launch services for human space tourism
and satellites. The company produces space-capable launch vehicles such as Sub-Orbital and Orbital Reusable Rockets. SpanTrik intends to renovate launch services for human space travel and payload in the near future by implementing advanced engineering and production systems via its new reusable launch Vehicle. We aim to provide a reliable, consistent approach to space at a nominal cost.
